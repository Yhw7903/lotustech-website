About LotusTech

An Australian pharmaceutical microbiology company supplying reference cultures and sterilisation validation products to customers in Australia and internationally.

Who we are

Precision, Compliance, Partnership

LotusTech Pty Ltd

LotusTech is an Australian pharmaceutical microbiology company supplying quantitative reference cultures under the VerisQ brand. Our products incorporate NCTC and NCPF reference strains sourced from UKHSA Culture Collections under licence, formulated as lyophilised pellets for non-therapeutic pharmaceutical quality control testing.

We serve pharmaceutical manufacturers, contract laboratories, and research institutions across Australia and internationally, providing authenticated reference cultures with full pharmacopoeial traceability and regulatory documentation.

MicroDome Partnership

LotusTech is a co-founder of MicroDome Labs — a science-driven manufacturer dedicated to microbial control solutions for pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device sectors. Founded in 2016 and headquartered in China with an R&D presence in Australia, MicroDome combines global manufacturing capability with agile product development.

As the authorised Australian representative, LotusTech provides local technical support, regulatory guidance, and supply chain management for MicroDome biological indicators, chemical indicators, and reference cultures.

Our Purpose

To supply authenticated, quantitative reference materials and sterilisation validation products that meet international pharmacopoeial standards — maintaining the highest levels of regulatory compliance, scientific traceability, and customer support for the pharmaceutical and medical device industries.

Australian Regulatory Compliance

TGA Registration & Therapeutic Goods Framework

VerisQ Reference Cultures

VerisQ reference cultures are non-therapeutic pharmaceutical quality control materials and are not classified as therapeutic goods under the TGA framework. We maintain GMP-aligned quality management practices across all aspects of storage, handling, and supply:

  • Batch-level documentation and COA issuance
  • Cold-chain management for lyophilised materials
  • Documented traceability to authenticated reference strains
  • Quality management aligned to ISO 9001 standards

MicroDome Biological Indicators

LotusTech is the TGA-registered Australian sponsor for MicroDome biological indicators, which are regulated as Medical Device Class 1 products under the Australian Register of Therapeutic Goods (ARTG):

  • ARTG 455431 — VHP Biological Indicator (active since July 2024)
  • ARTG 465729 — Steam & Dry Heat Biological Indicator (active since October 2024)

All MicroDome indicators supplied by LotusTech comply with Australian medical device classification requirements and relevant ISO sterilisation standards.

MicroDome holds Certifications, Accreditations & Regulatory Registrations including ISO 9001, ISO 17025, and ISO 13485 — full details and downloadable documents are available on the MicroDome product page.

Strain Origin & Authorisation

Strains Origin

UKHSA Culture Collections Licence

The manufacturing and supply licence for the NCTC strains used in this product has been formally granted by the UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA).

LotusTech holds a Material Licence Agreement with UKHSA Culture Collections (Porton Down, UK) covering the following authenticated reference strains used in VerisQ products:

  • NCTC 10788 — Staphylococcus aureus
  • NCTC 12924 — Pseudomonas aeruginosa
  • NCTC 12923 — Escherichia coli
  • NCTC 12935 — Clostridium sporogenes
  • NCTC 10400 — Bacillus subtilis subsp.
  • NCPF 3179 — Candida albicans
  • NCPF 2275 — Aspergillus brasiliensis
  • NCTC 6017 — Salmonella enterica subsp. enterica serovar Abony
  • NCTC 13227 — Burkholderia cenocepacia
  • NCTC 10743 — Burkholderia cepacia
  • NCTC 13007 — Burkholderia multivorans

Authorised Logo Use

The Culture Collections of the UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) has formally authorised LotusTech to display the NCTC and NCPF logos for non-promotional and informational purposes. These logos are displayed here in a neutral and factual context and do not imply endorsement or approval by UKHSA.
