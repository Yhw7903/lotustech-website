Biological Indicators

Process Monitoring

High-performance biological indicators with rapid readout times and defined spore populations for validating sterilisation efficacy.

  • Steam sterilisation (121°C, 132°C)
  • Ethylene oxide (EO)
  • Hydrogen peroxide vapour (VPHP)
  • Dry heat sterilisation
  • ISO 11138 compliant formats
  • Rapid readout designs available

Chemical Indicators

Visual Monitoring

Colour-change indicators for at-a-glance process monitoring and documentation of sterilisation cycle completion.

  • Class 1: Process indicators
  • Class 2–6: Specialized indicators
  • Steam, EO, and VPHP formats
  • External and internal placement
  • ISO 11135 compliant designs
  • Archival documentation support

About the Brand

MicroDome Labs: Science-driven microbial control

Founded in 2016 — Global expertise, local support

MicroDome Labs is a science-driven manufacturer dedicated to microbial control solutions for the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device sectors. Headquartered in China with an expert R&D team in Australia, MicroDome combines global technical capability with agile manufacturing to help clients meet the highest sterility assurance standards.

LotusTech is the co-founder and authorised Australian representative of MicroDome Labs, providing local technical support, regulatory guidance, and supply chain management for customers in Australia and internationally.

All MicroDome products are manufactured to ISO 9001:2015 quality standards and comply with ISO 11135 and ISO 11138 sterilisation validation requirements.
